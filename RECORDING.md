# Three-minute recording path

Open `http://127.0.0.1:4173`.

1. Read the hero and click **Start the three-minute lab**.
2. Show the frozen task, then click **Open the harness**.
3. Click **Remove waste** and enable all four context controls.
4. Click **Choose an engine**. Show MLX-LM for one private user, then click **One hundred users** to show vLLM on Nebius.
5. Click **Run sponsors** and click all three **Run now** buttons. Butterbase stores the demo receipt, EverOS retrieves learner memory, and Nebius returns the vLLM replay.
6. Click **Open inference** and trace text → tokens → prefill → KV → decode → HBM.
7. Click **Use memory**, then **Apply recalled memory**. Read the changed engine-selection instruction.
8. Click **Verify the result** and show the passing local gates plus explicit demo-replay boundaries.

Close on: “Start with the agent you already use. End by understanding and optimizing the inference system that runs it.”
